## Sign Up and Log In

Get started by creating your Hexus account. Hexus uses email-based authentication for simplicity.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit Hexus" icon="globe">
    Open your browser and navigate to [hexus.ai](https://hexus.ai).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Register" icon="user-plus">
    Click **Sign Up**. Enter your email, create a strong password, and verify your account via the confirmation email.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log In" icon="log-in">
    Return to the login page, enter your credentials, and access your dashboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="tip">
  Enable two-factor authentication in your account settings for added security.
</Callout>

## Create Your First Demo or Video

Choose between creating a demo or video. Both leverage AI to produce professional content from simple inputs.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Interactive Demo" icon="play-circle">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Select Template" icon="layout">
        Click **Create New** > **Demo**. Pick a product demo template.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Input Details" icon="edit-3">
        Enter your product URL, key features, and target audience.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Generate & Publish" icon="zap">
        Click **Generate**. Preview, then publish or embed.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Video Guide" icon="video">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Choose Format" icon="film">
        Select **Create New** > **Video**. Choose tutorial or explainer style.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Add Script" icon="file-text">
        Paste your script or let AI generate one from bullet points.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Render & Share" icon="share-2">
        Render the video, download, or share via link.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Embed Your Content

Share your new content by embedding it on your site. Use the generated code snippet.

<CodeGroup tabs="HTML,React">
```html
<iframe src="https://hexus.ai/embed/your-demo-id" width="100%" height="500" frameborder="0"></iframe>
```
```jsx
import { HexusEmbed } from '@hexus/embed';

<HexusEmbed demoId="your-demo-id" height={500} />
```
</CodeGroup>

<Expandable title="Troubleshooting Common Issues" default-open="false">
  - **Generation stuck?** Check your quota in settings.
  - **Embed not loading?** Ensure `your-demo-id` matches the ID from your library.
</Expandable>
